 The design is a modal dialog for canceling an order. It contains the following components:

- A header with the title "Cancel Order #1010" and a close button.
- A body with the order details, including the order number, date, shipping address, and item details.
- A section for the cancellation summary, including the reason for cancellation and the option to send a notification to the customer.
- A section for the refund amount and the option to refund the customer.
- A footer with two buttons: "Keep order" and "Cancel order".

The actions that can be performed are:

- Clicking the close button will close the dialog without canceling the order.
- Clicking the "Keep order" button will close the dialog and keep the order active.
- Clicking the "Cancel order" button will cancel the order and refund the customer.
