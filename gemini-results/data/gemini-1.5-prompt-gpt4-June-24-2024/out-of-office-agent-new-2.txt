## UX Component Breakdown and Actions:

This design showcases a section for managing agent availability, likely within a customer support or similar platform. 

1. Navigation Tabs:

 Component: Four tabs at the top: "Agent Availability", "Apply OOO", "Apply for an agent", "Settings"
 Position: Top of the section, horizontally aligned.
 Style: Simple text, likely with a visual indicator (underline, different color) for the active tab.
 Action: Clicking on a tab would likely navigate to the corresponding section within agent availability management.

2. Section Title:

 Component: Text "APPLY OUT OF OFFICE"
 Position: Below the navigation tabs, left aligned.
 Style: Bold text, larger font size than other text elements.
 Action:  Informational, indicating the purpose of the form below.

3. Email Input Field:

 Component: Textbox with label "Email" and a magnifying glass icon.
 Position: Below the section title, left aligned.
 Style: Standard textbox with a light grey background, the label is above the textbox.
 Action: Allows the user to input an email address. The magnifying glass icon might suggest a search functionality, but without further context, it's unclear.

4. Date Range Picker:

 Component: Two textboxes labeled "From" and "To" with calendar icons.
 Position: Below the email input field, "From" on the left and "To" on the right.
 Style: Standard textboxes with light grey background, each with a calendar icon on the right.
 Action: Clicking on the textboxes or the calendar icons would likely open a calendar widget to select a date range.

5. Apply Button:

 Component: Button labeled "Apply"
 Position: Below the date range picker, centered.
 Style:  Button with a solid background color, contrasting with the background of the page.
 Action:  Clicking this button would likely submit the entered information (email, date range) to apply the out-of-office status.
